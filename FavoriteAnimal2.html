E W O K S

A Star Wars Character

Concept & Creations

While George Lucas was creating Return of the Jedi, he had an idea to create a primitive species take down the technologically advanced Empire. At first, he wanted to have the Wookies take down the Empire, but he realized that the Wookies would later be technologically skilled. Due to this, he created the Ewoks. Because Wookies are tall, he made Ewoks short.

Ewoks are named after the Miwok, a Native American tribe.

Appearances

Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi

History

When the Empire took control of the Moon of Endor, they underestimated their abilites and paid no attention to them, as they were primitive creatures.

Ewoks

From the Star Wars universe, Ewoks are from the Forest Moon of Endor. Standing at only one meter tall, Ewoks are primitive creatures that joined forces with the Rebel Alliance to defeat the imperial ship generator. They helped greatly for the Rebel Alliance to win the Batlle of Endor.
